## üé¨ **Animation Narrative Flow (From Original Plan)**

### Section Transitions
1. **Hero ‚Üí About:** Title fades with scale, manifesto lines fade in sequentially
2. **About ‚Üí Projects:** Blocks collapse with rotation, Projects title expands
3. **Projects ‚Üí Skills:** Cards slide out, tech icons fade in
4. **Skills ‚Üí Timeline:** Icons scatter, timeline draws in
5. **Timeline ‚Üí Blog:** Timeline fades, blog cards rise up
6. **Blog ‚Üí Contact:** Cards shrink, contact form scales in
7. **Contact:** Waves return (callback to hero), closing the journey

### Animation Principles
- **Bold typography as cinematic anchor**
- **Scroll = story** (entry + exit animations)
- **Parallax depth** (multiple layers)
- **Microinteractions** (hover, click, focus)
- **Consistent motion language**
- **Performance-conscious** (60fps target)
